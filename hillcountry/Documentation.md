## Section 2 : Navigating through the APP ##
- - - - - -

&nbsp;

This section of the document is going to explain in detail on how to download, sign in and add cameras to your **Hill Country Exotics** app on iPhone. Proceed with the steps given below:

&nbsp;

### Step 1 : Download the App ###

Go to the App store on your iPhone and type **"Hill Country Exotics"** in the search field. Now tap on the download button to install the App.

&nbsp;


<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/appstore1.png" width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

### Step 2 : Opening the App ###

Go to the phone menu and tap on the **"Hill Country Exotics"** App Icon.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/phonehome1.png" width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

### Step 3 : App Homepage ###

After clicking the App Icon, it will take you to the Hill Country Exotics home page.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/home.png?token=AlKC8rYiQdeFyx5L0i1hyxYcJScaDXU_ks5cEIlEwA%3D%3D" width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

### Step 4 : Sign In to the App ###

Fill in the Username (Email address associated with the account) and password details. After that tap on the sign in button.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/details1.png?token=AlKC8l4NPKt2-6gBIP2OQ0mbWGoIHxGaks5cEIydwA%3D%3D " width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

### Step 5 : Add the camera ###

After signing in, it takes you to the App homepage. Now tap on **"Add"** to add cameras to your
account.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/signin1.png?token=AlKC8kPSCHo4R3klN1K_SEHNNaH_EZC7ks5cERkLwA%3D%3D  " width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

### Step 6 : Create Camera by entering the details ###

Add the camera details (Camera Name: unique id provided to you (Here "WW-00001") , Camera Id: Hexa decimal provided 8 characters) and tap on **"Create"** to add camera
to your account.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/camera.jpeg?token=AlKC8occzrYoXNC7gOrAnVH97v85ASYwks5cER4BwA%3D%3D   " width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/add_camera1.png " width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

### Step 7 : Camera Added ###

Now the camera is added and it will be shown on the App with **"Camera Id"** as the name.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/addcamera.jpeg?token=AlKC8onGbIqp_A6HF3LEStug5vPmzwTAks5cESG8wA%3D%3D  " width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

### Step 8 : Play and stop the streaming ###

Tap on the camera (Here WW-00001) to go to the camera menu and Tap on the play button (Green button) to start streaming from the camera, tap stop button (red button) to stop streaming the camera.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/play1.png?token=AlKC8klrzuRVv8dt-LjsQkgml73HNyFTks5cESwZwA%3D%3D   " width="300" height="400" />

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/streaming1.png?token=AlKC8rfWEqR9OANFiYXfqtWihab9HsGlks5cETCbwA%3D%3D " width="300" height="400" />

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/snapshot.png?token=AlKC8vpnSuyxEZTkaxdDaWi3GkZqKw7tks5cETYQwA%3D%3D  " width="300" height="400" />

## Section 3 : Recovering the password ##

- - - - -

&nbsp;

If you forgot the password then tap on **"Forgot your password?"**.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/password1.png " width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

After that, it will take you to the following screen where you have to enter your account email address and tap on **"Forgot Password"**.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/password2.png " width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

Now enter the **"CONFIRMATION CODE"** that you got on your **mobile number** and type in a **"New Password"** for your account. After that, tap **"Update Password"**.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/password3.png" width="300" height="400" />\

&nbsp;

With this you have successfully update your account password. Now you can sign in to App like usual.**(Refer Step 4 of Section 2)**

## Section 5 : Inactive account and Unable to add cameras? ##
- - - - - -

&nbsp;

### Step 1 : Adding camera ###

When a new account is created and if it is inactive, you can see the **"Account is inactive"** message on the homepage.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/inactiveapp.png" width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

When you try to add a camera to an inactive account, it will not let you add and prompts an error message.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/inactiveapp1.png" width="300" height="400" />

&nbsp;

### Step 2 : Updating payment method on web portal ###

Go to web portal and sign in to the inactive account. click on cancel button (Red box with white cross sign inside) on the homepage and proceed to payment update by
tapping on **UPDATE** button.

&nbsp;


<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/cancel_login.png" width="800" height="400" />

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/update_login.png" width="800" height="400" />

&nbsp;

### Step 3 : Adding new payment method ##

Tap on **NEW** to add a new payment method.

&nbsp;


<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/new_login.png" width="800" height="400" />

&nbsp;

Fill the payment form with valid details and submit it.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/pay_submit.png" width="800" height="400" />

&nbsp;

Go to Subscriptions on the homepage navigation.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/cameras_add.png" width="800" height="400" />

&nbsp;

Select the number of cameras, payment method and **UPDATE** them to add cameras to your account.

&nbsp;

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hatboysoftware/wildlife-dominion-doc/master/hillcountry/camera_add.png" width="800" height="400" />
